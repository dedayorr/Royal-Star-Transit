About Us

Because Your Life Matters

At Royal Star Transit, we provide reliable, efficient and cost-effective non-emergency transportation services.

Wellness That Fits Your Life

We understand securing transportation for you or a loved one for a medical or non-medical purposes could be a daunting task. With Royal Star Transit, you can sit back and relax, we will take care of you or your loved one. We provide door-to- door services for clients who are mobile and clients who are wheelchair bound. All out vehicles are registered, and ADA approved. Our drivers are cautious, professional and highly trained to operate our vehicles. We pride ourselves on always being punctual to pick you up at your scheduled location and drop you off at your desired destination on time and safely. At Royal Star Transit, all appointments are important either medical or non-medical. We also understand that a missed medical appointment could be detrimental to the client’s health. And with that fact, we made it our mission and priority to get you to your appointment safely and conveniently. With us, you are guaranteed a ride with a peace of mind.

Our Mission

To provide you with a unique, cost effective, and reliable transportation solution that meets individual needs while accommodating facility policies and concerns.
